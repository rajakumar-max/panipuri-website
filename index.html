About

Started in 2007, our journey has been sumple and focused - serve great panipuri with uncompromised hygiene.


While taste brings people once, consistency and cleaniness are what make them return. From day one , we have followed strict hygiene practices , using fresh ingredients prepared daily and maintaining a clean serving environment ar all times.


Over the years, this discipline has earned us the trust of thousands of happy customers, manu of whom have been visiting us regularly for years. We don't experiment with shortcuts , and we don't change what works - balanced taste, clean preparation , and honest service.


We believe street food can be both delicious and hygienic , and we prove it every single day at our stall.
